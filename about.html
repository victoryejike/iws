our process of web designing

01UNDERSTANDING THE IDEA

We believe that communication and understanding is the foundation of every company-client relationship. Our team focuses on writing down each and every requirement and idea about the client’s project and understanding it in depth.

02 COMBINING THE INPUTS

We put down all the points together to get a better idea about the targets that we have to achieve in the mentioned time frame along with the results that we have to show to stand out in the competitive market.

03 CREATING BLUEPRINTS

Our team works together to make the outline and the base website that can be changed anytime. This strategy has worked for all the projects we did in the past.

04 DESIGNING BLUEPRINTS

The outline is then designed as an initial product that can be shown to the client and to get an idea about how the final product will come out to be.

05 CLIENT’S FEEDBACK

We involve the client in the process of designing because his ideas and opinions matter to us the most. If he wants any changes after looking at the website, we will implement them immediately.

06TESTING AND PUBLISHING

After all the changes are made, we then pass the website through multiple testing sites to make sure that the website is ready to publish without any flaw in it. This published website is then delivered to the client.

07 COUNTLESS REVISIONS FOR 15 DAYS

We don’t leave our clients just like that, we offer them a free maintenance of their website for the next 15 days so that they can be happy and completely satisfied by our work.
