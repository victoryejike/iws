Visual-Effects

Visual effects is the process by which imagery is created or manipulated outside the context of a live action shot in filmmaking. The integration of live action footage and CG elements to create realistic imagery is called VFX. They help us to simulate the imagined events in a story or imagined world with illusions or tricks of the eye. Well-executed VFX can be on brand, on message, and really elevate your content.

Rotoscoping & Chroma Keying

Rotoscoping is an animation technique that animators use to trace over motion picture footage, frame by frame, to produce realistic action. Originally, animators projected photographed live-action movie images onto a glass panel and traced over the image. Chroma Keying is much better and less work and is used for the same purpose. But rotoscoping is said to be more authentic on a greater level.

2D Animation

2D animation is a traditional animation method that has existed since the late 1800s. It is one drawing followed by another in a slightly different pose, followed by another in a slightly different pose, so on and so on, at 24 frames per second. This process goes on and on to make it look like a 2-dimensional object in a form of an image.

3D Animation

3D Animations are the latest form of computer graphics and the most used as well. It animates objects that appear in a three-dimensional space. They can be moved and rotated like real objects. It is at the heart of games and virtual reality, but it may also be used in tricking the eye in presenting visual graphics.

3D Modelling

3D Modelling is very unique and not very found due to the complexity of it but we are launching our service to make 3d models as well. It is the process of developing mathematical representation of any surface of an object in three dimensions via specialized softwares. Three types of 3D modeling are supported: wireframe, surface, and solid. Each type has its own creation and editing techniques.

Character Animation

Event design is probably the most focused thing in today’s world. Character animation is a specialized area of the animation process, which involves bringing animated characters into life. The role of the Character Animator is almost the same of a film maker or a stage actor who is presenting as an actor. Character animations give life to an actor drawn by a pencil.
